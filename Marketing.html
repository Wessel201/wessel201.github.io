The 4 p's of marketing

Before you can start advertising your product you should have a good understanding of the 4 p’s. The 4 p’s also know as the marketing mix is a concept conceived by professor marketing Jerome Mccarthy in 1960. The concept of the 4 p’s has expanded to the 7p’s but 3 are not relevant unless your run a larger company. The original 4p’s are Product, Price, Place, Promotion, additionally the 7p’s also consist of Personnel, Process, Physical evidence.
